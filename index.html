A.P. Moller - Maersk is an integrated container logistics company. Connecting and simplifying trade to help customers grow and thrive. With a dedicated team of over 76,000, operating in 130 countries, we go all the way to enable global trade for a growing world. With simple end-to-end offering of products and digital services, seamless customer engagement and a superior end-to-end delivery network, Maersk enables its customers to trade and grow by transporting goods anywhere - all over the world. As the global integrator of container logistics, Maersk is composed of the following segments: Ocean, Logistics & Services, Terminals & Towage and Manufacturing & Others. OCEAN includes the ocean activities (Maersk, Safmarine, and Sealand A Maersk Company) together with Hamburg Sd brands (Hamburg Sd and Aliana) as well as strategic transshipment hubs under the APM Terminals brand (Rotterdam, Maasvlakte II, Algeciras, Tangiers, Tangier-Med II, Port Said and joint ventures in Salalah, Tanjung Pelepas and Bremerhaven). LOGISTICS & SERVICES has five main activities: supply chain management, intermodal, inland services, freight forwarding and other services that include warehousing, distribution, and other value-added services as well as trade finance. TERMINALS & TOWAGE includes gateway terminals involving landside activities being port activities where the customers are mainly the carriers, and towage services under the Svitzer brand. MANUFACTURING & OTHERS includes the activities of Maersk Container Industry with the production and sale of reefer containers and dry containers. Others include the third-party activities of Maersk Oil Trading and bulk activities taken over as part of the Hamburg Sd transaction. However, these bulk activities were divested in January 2019.
